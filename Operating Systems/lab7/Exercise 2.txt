First fit:
-Advantages:
 +Simple
 +Tends to produce larger free blocks toward the end of the addess space
-Disadvantages:
 +Fragmentation

Best fit:
-Advantages:
 +Works well when most allocation are of small size
 +Reletively simple
-Disadvantages:
 +External fragmentation
 +Slow allocation
 +Slow deallocation
 +Tends to produce many useless tiny fragments

Worst fit:
-Advantages:
 +Works best if allocations are of medium sizes
-Disadvantages:
 +External fragmentation
 +Tends to break large free blocks such that large partitions cannot be allocated
